How It Works

Our platform ensures transparency and builds trust by connecting institutes, donors, and suppliers in a secure ecosystem.

Institutes Raise Requirements

Orphanages, elderly homes, and food providers specify their needs through the platform.

Donors Choose Contributions

Donors select specific items and quantities to donate, like "10kg of rice" rather than cash.

Suppliers Fulfill Orders

Local shops receive payments, prepare items, and deliver them directly to institutes.

Trust & Transparency

We've built safeguards into our platform to ensure donations are used properly and efficiently.

No Direct Cash Transfers

Money is sent directly to verified suppliers, ensuring funds are used only for intended purposes.

Anti-Cartelization Measures

Large donations are distributed among different shops, and consumption patterns are monitored to prevent fraud.
